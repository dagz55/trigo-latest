# TriGo Trider Guide

Welcome, Trider! This guide explains how to use the TriGo app to find passengers and manage your rides.

## Key Features for Triders

- **Online/Offline Toggle**: Easily switch your availability status to start or stop receiving ride requests
- **Live Map View**: See your current location and active ride details on an interactive map
- **Ride Management**:
  - View pending ride requests with pickup/drop-off locations and fare details
  - Accept rides and track their progress
  - Mark rides as picked up and completed
  - See ride history and status updates
- **Location Tracking**: Automatic location updates while online to help passengers find you
- **Real-time Updates**: Instant notifications for new ride requests and status changes
- **Ride Details**:
  - Clear pickup and drop-off location information
  - Estimated fare amount
  - Request timing information
  - Navigation assistance

1. **Login**: Access the Trider dashboard at http://localhost:3000/trider/dashboard using your credentials:

   Or
   ```

   Password: Trider123!
   ```

2. **Go Online**:
   - Click the "Go Online" toggle switch in the top-right corner
   - Ensure your location services are enabled when prompted
   - Your status will change to "Online" and you'll be added to the queue

3. **Accept Rides**:
   - View incoming ride requests in the left panel
   - Each request shows:
     - Pickup and drop-off locations
     - Fare amount
     - Request timing
   - Click "Accept Ride" to take a request

4. **Manage Active Rides**:
   - Once accepted, follow the map to the pickup location
   - Click "Pickup Passenger" when you've collected them
   - Navigate to the drop-off point
   - Click "Complete Ride" upon reaching the destination

5. **Go Offline**:
   - Complete any active rides first
   - Toggle your status to "Offline" when done
   - You'll stop receiving new requests

## Tips

- Keep your location services enabled for accurate tracking
- Complete rides promptly to maintain good service
- Monitor the live map for better navigation
- Stay online during your committed hours
- Keep your app updated for the best experience

## Support

If you encounter any issues:
- Contact your dispatcher through the provided contact number
- Ensure your internet connection is stable
- Check if your location services are enabled
- Restart the app if experiencing technical difficulties
